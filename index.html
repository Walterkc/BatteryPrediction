Battery Case

Our assignment was to predict whether a car would start or not, with this question came some variables like: the nature (temperature), Ambient temperature, battery power output capacity, power requirements of the vehicle, battery material, state of charge. With this prediction Crossyn could alert you the night before the starting failure about the problem and with this they could automatically send someone by to help you get on your way.

Prediction

SVM

One of the most important battery parameters is the battery state-of-charge (SOC). Knowing the amount of energy left in a battery compared with the energy it had when it was full, gives the user an indication of how much longer a battery will continue to perform before it needs recharging. SVM techniques (SVMs) have developed greatly in recent years, giving rise to a new kind of learning machines that use the central concept of SVMs, namely, kernels, for a number of learning tasks. In this research study, SVM is used as automated learning tool to successfully predict the SOC of a high capacity battery cell as a function of cell voltage, cell current and cell temperature. This will predict if the car will start or not.
